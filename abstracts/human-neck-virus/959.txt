[Papillomavirus and head and neck cancer].

Tobacco and alcohol consumption are the main risk factors for head and neck
cancers. Papillomavirus (HPV) infection was recently associated with the
development of malignant tumors of the oropharynx, according to molecular and
biological arguments. We describe the oncogenic mechanisms of HPV infections, the
epidemiological and clinical aspects of associated head and neck cancers, their
prognosis, and issues of specific therapeutic strategies.
