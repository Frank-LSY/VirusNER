Gene therapy in dentistry: present and future.

Baum BJ.

Gene therapy is one of several novel biological treatments under active study for
a wide variety of clinical applications, including many relevant to dentistry.
This review will provide some background on this therapeutic approach, assess the
current state of its applications generally, and in the oral cavity, and suggest 
the implications for its use in the next 25 years.
