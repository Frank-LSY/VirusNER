This article is a continuation of the "Do You Know Your Guidelines" series, an
initiative of the American Head and Neck Society's Education Committee to
increase awareness of current best practices pertaining to head and neck cancer. 
The National Comprehensive Cancer Network guidelines for the management of
nasopharyngeal cancer are reviewed here in a systematic fashion. These guidelines
outline the workup, treatment and surveillance of patients with nasopharyngeal
cancer. © 2016 Wiley Periodicals, Inc. Head Neck 39: 201-205, 2017.
