New Avian Hepadnavirus in Palaeognathous Bird, Germany.

Jo WK, Pfankuche VM, Petersen H, Frei S, Kummrow M, Lorenzen S, Ludlow M, Metzger
J, Baumg√§rtner W, Osterhaus A, van der Vries E.

In 2015, we identified an avian hepatitis B virus associated with hepatitis in a 
group of captive elegant-crested tinamous (Eudromia elegans) in Germany. The
full-length genome of this virus shares <76% sequence identity with other
avihepadnaviruses. The virus may therefore be considered a new extant avian
hepadnavirus.
